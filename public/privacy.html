Privacy Policy

Easy Forty is a personal referral program operated by an individual (the “Program”). We collect only the information you provide: your mobile phone number and your payout handle (e.g., Venmo or Zelle).

What we do with your data

  • Send you up to three SMS/MMS messages to deliver your referral link and guide you through verification.
  • Record your completion status and store your proof screenshot URL.
  • Notify the program operator to send your payout.

Data retention

Phone numbers and payout handles are retained for up to 12 months for fraud prevention and auditing. Screenshot media links provided by our SMS provider may be ephemeral; we store only their URLs and basic metadata.

Your choices

Reply STOP to any message to opt out. Reply HELP for help. To delete your data, email support@easyforty.com with your phone number.
